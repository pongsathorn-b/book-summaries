# Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

Curiosity about why talented people often fail while mediocre-but-persistent people succeed. The book challenges the talent myth head-on.

## Key Takeaways

- Grit = passion + perseverance — sustained interest over years
- Effort counts twice: talent × effort = skill; skill × effort = achievement
- Interest must be developed, not just found — 'play in the beginning'
- Purpose matters: connecting your work to something beyond yourself
- The 'hard way' zone — deliberate practice with clear goals

## Actionable Lessons

- Find a 'hard goal' and work toward it every single day
- Develop rituals that maintain your grit practice regardless of mood
- Cultivate purpose by asking 'Who benefits from my work?'
- Keep a 'grit journal' — track daily effort toward long-term projects
- Seek feedback and treat setbacks as data, not verdicts
