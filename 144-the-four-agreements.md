# The Four Agreements

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

The idea that most suffering is self-generated through agreement with others' beliefs — and that you can renegotiate those agreements — felt liberating.

## Key Takeaways

- Agreement 1: Be impeccable with your word — speak with integrity; don't spread gossip or self-hatred
- Agreement 2: Don't take anything personally — others' actions reflect their own reality, not you
- Agreement 3: Don't make assumptions — ask questions, clarify expectations
- Agreement 4: Always do your best — not perfect, just what you can on that day
- Most suffering comes from 'dream of the planet' — collective agreements we've absorbed

## Actionable Lessons

- Catch yourself before gossiping — is it true, kind, necessary?
- When hurt, ask: 'Is this about me or about them?'
- When you assume, state your interpretation and invite correction
- Set a daily intention: 'My best looks different today — and that's fine'
- Notice how often you seek external validation and question it
