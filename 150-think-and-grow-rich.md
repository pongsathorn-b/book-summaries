# Think and Grow Rich

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

Required reading in personal development circles for nearly a century. Wanted to understand the core ideas that shaped the modern self-help industry.

## Key Takeaways

- Desire: a burning, obsessive want for a specific outcome — not a wish
- Faith: visualize and affirm the outcome as already real
- Specialized knowledge: organize it into an actionable plan
- The mastermind: a group of like-minded people who sharpen each other
- Persistence: the refusal to quit when obstacles arise

## Actionable Lessons

- Write out your definite major purpose in a clear, specific statement
- Create a mastermind group of 2–5 people with complementary skills
- Use autosuggestion: daily affirmations and visualization of your goal
- Take action daily toward your major purpose, even small steps count
- Review and revise your plan monthly based on results
