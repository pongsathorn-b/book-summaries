# The 5 AM Club: Own Your Morning, Elevate Your Life

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

Elite performers across domains share early-rising habits. Wanted to understand and implement the science and structure behind it.

## Key Takeaways

- The 20/20/20 formula: 20 min exercise, 20 min reflection, 20 min learning
- Sleep quality matters more than sleep quantity — 5 AM wake aligns with circadian rhythm
- Morning solitude before the world intrudes creates clarity and intention
- Habit stacking: attach new morning habits to existing routines
- Protecting the 5 AM window means saying no to late-night entertainment

## Actionable Lessons

- Set a consistent bedtime — sleep before midnight is more restorative
- Start with one 20-minute block (movement) and build from there
- Use the 'reflection' block for journaling: gratitude, goals, visualization
- The learning block: read, listen to a podcast, or study your craft
- No phone, no email — guard the first hour from external intrusion
