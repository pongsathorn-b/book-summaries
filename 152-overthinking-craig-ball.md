# Overthinking: A Guide to Break the Habit and Live Freely

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

Most people overthink rather than underthink — and overthinking paralyzes action. Wanted concrete tools to interrupt the cycle.

## Key Takeaways

- Overthinking = rumination (past-focused) + worry (future-focused)
- Cognitive distortions: catastrophizing, all-or-nothing, mind-reading
- Action breaks the cycle — imperfect action beats perfect analysis
- Set a 'decision deadline': make the call by X date, then commit
- The 10-10-10 rule: how will you feel in 10 minutes, 10 months, 10 years?

## Actionable Lessons

- Notice overthinking triggers: specific topics, times of day, social contexts
- Use 'thought challenging': is this fact, opinion, or distortion?
- Set a 2-minute rule: if a decision takes under 2 minutes, decide immediately
- Schedule a daily 'worry time' — contain worry rather than suppressing it
- Move your body — physical action interrupts rumination chemically
