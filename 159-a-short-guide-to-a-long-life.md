# A Short Guide to a Long Life

> "Your potential is shaped by your daily habits and the frameworks you use to understand yourself and the world."

## Why I Read It

Health is the foundation of everything else — wanted a practical, evidence-based overview of what actually moves the needle on longevity.

## Key Takeaways

- The biggest longevity factors: not smoking, moderate alcohol, regular exercise, quality sleep
- Caloric restriction and fasting windows improve metabolic health and longevity markers
- Social connection is as important as diet and exercise for lifespan
- Stress management (meditation, nature) significantly reduces all-cause mortality
- Regular health screenings catch disease early — prevention is cheaper than cure

## Actionable Lessons

- Walk 8,000+ steps daily — even moderate movement dramatically reduces health risks
- Sleep 7-9 hours in a consistent schedule — prioritize sleep like a performance drug
- Practice intermittent fasting: 16:8 window reduces inflammation and improves metabolism
- Build one social ritual per week — maintain connections that predict longevity
- Annual check-ups: blood work, blood pressure, cancer screenings by age
