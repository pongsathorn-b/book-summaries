# Thinking, Fast and Slow

## Summary

Daniel Kahneman explores the two systems that drive how we think: fast, intuitive System 1 and slow, deliberate System 2. Understanding their interplay reveals why we make predictable irrational choices.

## Key Takeaways

- System 1 (fast thinking) is prone to predictable biases.
- System 2 (slow thinking) is lazy and often defers to System 1.
- Knowing when to trust your gut and when to deliberate is itself a skill.
