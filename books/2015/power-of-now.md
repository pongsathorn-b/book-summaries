# The Power of Now

## Summary

Eckhart Tolle argues that most human suffering stems from living in the past or future rather than the present moment. His guide to spiritual awakening has become a touchstone for mindfulness literature.

## Key Takeaways

- Most psychological suffering comes from living in the past or future.
- The present moment is the only place life actually happens.
- Observing your egoic mind without attachment dissolves its power over you.
