# Modern Romance: An Investigation

## Summary

Aziz Ansari and sociologist Eric Klinenberg dig into modern romance, interviewing hundreds of people across cities to understand how technology, urbanization, and changing gender norms have transformed how we fall in love and stay together. It's both rigorously researched and wickedly funny.

## Key Takeaways

- Technology has fundamentally changed dating — more options but less commitment.
- Urbanization and changing gender roles reshape what love looks like.
- Humor and vulnerability are not opposites in modern relationships.
