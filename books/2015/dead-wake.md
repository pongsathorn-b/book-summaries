# Dead Wake: The Last Crossing of the Lusitania

## Summary

Erik Larson recounts the final voyage of the Lusitania in 1915, tracing the decisions and coincidences that led to its sinking by a German U-boat. He paints a vivid portrait of a world on the brink of war.

## Key Takeaways

- The Lusitania sinking was preventable — warnings were ignored for commercial and political reasons.
- German U-boat warfare represented a new, terrifying mode of conflict.
- Individual decisions and coincidences shape major historical events.
