# The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up

## Summary

Marie Kondo's decluttering philosophy centers on keeping only items that spark joy, and discarding the rest with gratitude. Her KonMari method became a global phenomenon for transforming both homes and minds.

## Key Takeaways

- Keep only items that spark joy; discard the rest with gratitude.
- The KonMari method works because it is as much psychological as physical.
- Decluttering your space is a gateway to decluttering your mind.
