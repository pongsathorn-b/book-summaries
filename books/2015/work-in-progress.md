# A Work in Progress

## Summary

Connor Franta's memoir chronicles his journey from small-town Minnesota to internet fame, exploring identity, sexuality, and what it means to grow up publicly.

## Key Takeaways

- Public identity formation is complicated when it happens online.
- Authenticity and performance are often in tension for public figures.
- Coming of age in the social media era has unique psychological challenges.
