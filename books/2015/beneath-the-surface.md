# Beneath the Surface: Killer Whales, SeaWorld, and the Truth Beyond Blackfish

## Summary

John Hargrove, a former SeaWorld trainer, reveals the dark reality of keeping orcas in captivity and the movement that Blackfish helped spark. The book exposes the gap between public spectacle and animal welfare.

## Key Takeaways

- Captivity fundamentally harms intelligent marine mammals like orcas.
- Public perception of marine parks shifted dramatically after investigative reporting.
- Animal welfare science has reshaped our ethical obligations to captive animals.
