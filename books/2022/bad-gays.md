# Bad Gays: A Homosexual History

## Summary

Huw Lemmey traces the untold history of gay subculture in the 20th century, exploring how queer identity was shaped by both criminalization and community-building.

## Key Takeaways

- Queer history has been systematically erased from mainstream archives.
- Community spaces were simultaneously sites of oppression and liberation.
- Understanding the past makes visible the present's own forms of hidden persecution.
