# I'm Glad My Mom Died

## Summary

Jennette McCurdy's memoir about recovering from years of abuse and eating disorders after her mother's influence is both devastating and darkly funny.

## Key Takeaways

- Enmeshment between mother and daughter can look like closeness from outside.
- Recovery requires confronting the dysfunction you were raised to normalize.
- Dark humor is a legitimate coping mechanism for processing genuine trauma.
