# The Rise and Fall of the Dinosaurs

## Summary

Steve Brusatte tells the story of the dinosaurs from their origins to their catastrophic end, using new fossil discoveries to rewrite what we thought we knew about these magnificent creatures.

## Key Takeaways

- Dinosaur extinction opened the door for mammalian dominance — we exist because of it.
- New fossil discoveries are rewriting our understanding of dinosaur biology and behavior.
- The K-Pg extinction was sudden by geological standards but gradual by human experience.
