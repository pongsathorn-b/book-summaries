# Atomic Habits

## Summary

James Clear's system for building better habits hinges on making small changes that compound over time: fall in love with the process, not the outcome, and identity-based habits create lasting change.

## Key Takeaways

- Small habits compound into remarkable results over time (1% better every day).
- The most effective habits are identity-based — they change who you believe yourself to be.
- Environment design often matters more than motivation or willpower.
