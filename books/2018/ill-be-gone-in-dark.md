# I'll Be Gone in the Dark

## Summary

Michelle McNamara's true crime investigation into the Golden State Killer combines obsessive research with personal vulnerability, tracking a serial predator through decades of near-misses.

## Key Takeaways

- True crime investigations often depend on persistence and obsession.
- The Golden State Killer evaded capture for decades partly through luck and geography.
- Digital DNA databases eventually solved cases that traditional policing could not.
