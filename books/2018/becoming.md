# Becoming

## Summary

Michelle Obama's memoir charts her journey from the South Side of Chicago to the White House, offering an intimate portrait of her life while in office and beyond.

## Key Takeaways

- Personal narrative is itself a political act for historically marginalized groups.
- The White House is both an institution and a home — managing both requires different skills.
- Grace and ambition are not mutually exclusive.
