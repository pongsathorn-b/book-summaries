# The Power of Habit

## Summary

Charles Duhigg reveals the neurological loop of cue, routine, reward that governs our daily habits, and how understanding this loop can help us change them.

## Key Takeaways

- The habit loop (cue-routine-reward) is exploitable for both formation and change.
- Keystone habits create cascade effects across many areas of life.
- Willpower is finite and depletable — environment design is more reliable.
