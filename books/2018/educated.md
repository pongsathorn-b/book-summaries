# Educated

## Summary

Tara Westover's memoir of growing up in a survivalist family in Idaho, never attending school, and eventually earning a PhD from Cambridge is a testament to the transformative power of education.

## Key Takeaways

- Education is both a literal lifeline and a source of profound grief.
- Family loyalty conflicts with the desire for independent thought.
- Intellectual growth is irreversible — you cannot unlearn what you come to understand.
