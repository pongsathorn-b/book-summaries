# 12 Rules for Life

## Summary

Jordan Peterson blends psychology, mythology, and philosophy into 12 principles for navigating chaos and finding meaning, drawn from his viral lectures and clinical practice.

## Key Takeaways

- Order and structure provide the conditions for genuine freedom.
- Meaningful suffering is preferable to comfortable nihilism.
- Personal responsibility is the foundation of navigating a chaotic world.
