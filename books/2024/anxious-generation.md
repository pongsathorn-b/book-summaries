# The Anxious Generation: How the Great Rewiring of Childhood Caused an Epidemic of Mental Illness

## Summary

Jonathan Haidt presents evidence that smartphone and social media use has rewired childhood, creating a mental health epidemic among Generation Z, and proposes concrete solutions for families and schools.

## Key Takeaways

- Smartphones and social media are rewiring developing brains in real time.
- The mental health crisis among teens, especially girls, is a public health emergency.
- Restricting phone use in schools and homes is the most actionable intervention available.
