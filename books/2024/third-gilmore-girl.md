# The Third Gilmore Girl: A Memoir

## Summary

Kelly Bishop shares the story of her unconventional life raising four daughters on a Christmas tree farm, her acting career, and the family dynamics behind Gilmore Girls.

## Key Takeaways

- Family mythology often obscures genuine dysfunction.
- Women's work — emotional and domestic — is systematically undervalued.
- Authenticity and likability are in constant tension for public women.
