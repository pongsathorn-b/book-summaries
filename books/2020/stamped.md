# Stamped: Racism, Antiracism, and You

## Summary

Jason Reynolds and Ibram X. Kendi present a history of American racism in accessible form for younger readers, separating racist from anti-racist ideas and showing how the battle between them shaped every era.

## Key Takeaways

- There is no neutral position on race — silence and inaction serve the status quo.
- Anti-racism is an active practice, not an inherited identity.
- Understanding history is essential to understanding present racial dynamics.
