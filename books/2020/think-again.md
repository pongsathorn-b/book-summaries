# Think Again

## Summary

Adam Grant makes the case for thinking like a scientist, regularly questioning our beliefs and updating our views rather than defending what we already think we know.

## Key Takeaways

- Intellectual humility is the most undervalued professional skill.
- The best thinkers update their views more frequently and more quickly than average.
- Persuasion works best when you understand the other person's mental model first.
