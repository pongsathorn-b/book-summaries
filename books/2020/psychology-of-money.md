# The Psychology of Money

## Summary

Morgan Housel uses stories to show that financial success is more about behavior than intelligence, exploring the psychological and emotional relationship we have with money.

## Key Takeaways

- Financial literacy is more about behavior than technical knowledge.
- Every financial decision involves emotional trade-offs that pure rationality ignores.
- Long-term wealth building requires patience and living below your means.
