# The Body Keeps the Score

## Summary

Bessel van der Kolk explores how trauma reshapes body and mind, showing why survivors of trauma often cannot verbalize their experiences and what therapeutic approaches actually help.

## Key Takeaways

- Trauma is stored in the body as much as in memory.
- Talk therapy alone is often insufficient for trauma recovery.
- The therapeutic relationship itself can be a vehicle for healing.
