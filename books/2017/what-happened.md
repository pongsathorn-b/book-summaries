# What Happened

## Summary

Hillary Clinton reflects on the 2016 election, her life in politics, and what it means to be a woman in power in an era of unprecedented scrutiny and division.

## Key Takeaways

- The 2016 election revealed deep structural divisions in American society.
- Being a woman in politics invites unique forms of scrutiny and attack.
- Understanding that election requires examining both personal and systemic factors.
