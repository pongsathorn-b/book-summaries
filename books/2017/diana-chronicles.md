# The Diana Chronicles

## Summary

Kate Moore reconstructs Princess Diana's life and legacy through extensive archival research and new interviews, separating myth from reality in the most scrutinized life of the 20th century.

## Key Takeaways

- Princess Diana was both a product and a prisoner of the royal system.
- Media coverage of her shaped public consciousness in unprecedented ways.
- Her legacy continues to influence conversations about mental health and humanitarianism.
