# The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

## Summary

Mark Manson argues that self-improvement is not about positive thinking but about learning to give fewer fks about things that don't matter, and focusing on what truly does.

## Key Takeaways

- Better mental health comes from caring about fewer, better things.
- Avoiding suffering is impossible; choosing what to suffer for is not.
- The self-help industry often profits from advice that actually increases anxiety.
