# The 48 Laws of Power

## Summary

Robert Greene distills centuries of political and social maneuvering into 48 laws, using historical examples from Bismarck to Madonna to illustrate how power actually works.

## Key Takeaways

- Power dynamics are present in every human relationship and institution.
- Strategic thinking is distinct from ethical thinking — Greene describes, not prescribes.
- Awareness of power games is itself a form of self-protection.
