# Girl, Stop Apologizing

## Summary

Rachel Hollis urges women to stop downplaying their ambitions and apologizing for wanting more, presenting a framework for owning your goals unapologetically.

## Key Takeaways

- Women are culturally conditioned to hedge their ambitions and apologize for wanting more.
- External validation is less satisfying than internal alignment with your goals.
- Action precedes motivation — start before you feel ready.
