# Over the Top

## Summary

Jonathan Van Ness reframes HIV as just one chapter in a life full of reinvention, from being a bullied teen to becoming a beloved TV personality and joy advocate.

## Key Takeaways

- Reinvention is possible at any stage of life.
- HIV is a manageable condition, not a defining one — reframing is powerful.
- Joy is a radical act in the face of systemic oppression.
