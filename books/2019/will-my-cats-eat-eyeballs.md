# Will My Cats Eat My Eyeballs?

## Summary

Caitlin Doughty answers the questions people are too afraid to ask about death, combining mortician expertise with mordant humor in bite-sized essays.

## Key Takeaways

- Death is ordinary — morticians and medical professionals see it as such.
- Humor and horror coexist naturally when confronting mortality.
- Cultural taboos around death deprive us of important knowledge and comfort.
