# The 5 Love Languages

## Summary

Gary Chapman's framework for understanding how different people give and receive love has become a relationship staple, identifying five primary languages: words, time, gifts, acts, and touch.

## Key Takeaways

- People give and receive love in fundamentally different ways.
- Miscommunication in relationships often stems from incompatible love languages.
- Speaking someone else's love language requires deliberate effort and observation.
