# The Covent Garden Ladies

## Summary

Hallie Rubenhold traces the lives of the women immortalized in the paintings that hang in the Tate, recovering the real stories behind the portraits of London's Victorian demi-monde.

## Key Takeaways

- The women of Georgian London's demi-monde had agency and influence within severe constraints.
- Historical records often erase the lives of women who didn't conform to respectable norms.
- Ruben's paintings of these women tell only part of their actual stories.
