# The Righteous Mind

## Summary

Jonathan Haidt explores why moral divides run so deep, arguing that our moral intuitions arise from evolved predispositions and that understanding them is key to bridging political divisions.

## Key Takeaways

- Moral intuition precedes moral reasoning — we feel our way to conclusions.
- Understanding your own moral foundations reduces unnecessary conflict.
- Political division reflects deep philosophical differences, not just ignorance.
