# The Anthropocene Reviewed: Essays on a Human-Centered Planet

## Summary

John Green turns his lens on the contemporary world, reviewing facets of modern life with his trademark wit and emotional depth in this collection of personal essays.

## Key Takeaways

- Paying deliberate attention to ordinary experiences is itself a form of creativity.
- Modern life contains both profound wonder and genuine absurdity.
- Self-awareness and humor are complementary rather than competing traits.
