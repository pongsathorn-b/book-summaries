# Will

## Summary

Will Smith's memoir covers his Philadelphia upbringing, his rise to global stardom, and the incident at the 2022 Oscars that forced a reckoning with his public persona.

## Key Takeaways

- Anger is a signal that something you care about is threatened.
- Public figures face impossible choices that private people never encounter.
- Apology without change is hollow; change without apology can still be meaningful.
