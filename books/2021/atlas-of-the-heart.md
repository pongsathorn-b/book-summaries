# Atlas of the Heart

## Summary

Brené Brown helps readers navigate the emotional complexity of finding meaning in an uncertain world, distinguishing between the small daily emotions and the big questions that shape our lives.

## Key Takeaways

- Meaning and purpose require ongoing cultivation, especially in uncertain times.
- Emotional courage — feeling fully rather than numbing — is the foundation of connection.
- Small daily choices accumulate into a life that either matches or contradicts your values.
