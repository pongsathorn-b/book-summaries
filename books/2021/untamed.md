# Untamed

## Summary

Glennon Doyle's memoir about leaving her marriage and discovering her true self became a rallying cry for women to stop performing and start living authentically.

## Key Takeaways

- Socialized femininity teaches women to manage men's comfort at the expense of their own.
- Breaking free from expected roles requires grieving the lost approval you counted on.
- Authentic living is more sustainable than performed perfection.
