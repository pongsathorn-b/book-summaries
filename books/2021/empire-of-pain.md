# Empire of Pain: The Secret History of the Sackler Dynasty

## Summary

Patrick Radden Keefe traces the origins and spread of the opioid crisis to the Sackler family and their company Purdue Pharma, exposing how a dynasty of philanthropy was built on the suffering of millions.

## Key Takeaways

- The opioid crisis was not an accident but a deliberate marketing strategy.
- Regulatory capture allowed Purdue Pharma to operate with impunity for decades.
- The Sackler family's philanthropy was used to launder their reputation while people died.
