# Poverty, by America

## Summary

Matthew Desmond investigates why poverty persists in the wealthiest nation on earth, arguing that the middle class benefits from the existence of a poor underclass.

## Key Takeaways

- The middle class benefits economically from the existence of a poor underclass.
- Poverty persists partly because solving it would require the comfortable to give something up.
- Shame is weaponized to prevent the poor from organizing collectively.
