# How to Kill a Witch

## Summary

Zoe Venditozzi and Claire Mitchell trace the history of witchcraft accusations and trials in Scotland, revealing how women were disproportionately targeted and the cultural legacy of that persecution.

## Key Takeaways

- Witch trial records reveal how communities policed women's behavior and sexuality.
- Scottish witch trials were part of a broader European moral panic.
- The women accused were often the most vulnerable members of their communities.
