# Everything Is Tuberculosis

## Summary

John Green profiles the global TB crisis, weaving his personal connection to the disease with the story of its deadly persistence in the age of COVID-19.

## Key Takeaways

- TB is the world's deadliest infectious disease and receives a fraction of COVID's attention.
- John Green's partnership with WHO has brought global health storytelling to new audiences.
- Historical patterns of epidemic response reveal what we still haven't learned.
