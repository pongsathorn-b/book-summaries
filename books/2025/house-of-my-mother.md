# The House of My Mother

## Summary

Shari Franke examines the dark reality behind her grandmother's internet-famous family, exploring abuse and dysfunction hidden beneath a wholesome public image.

## Key Takeaways

- Family influencers often build empires on foundations of concealed abuse.
- The gap between public performance and private reality is where trauma lives.
- Speaking out against family requires surviving the loss of the family narrative.
