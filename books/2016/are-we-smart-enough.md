# Are We Smart Enough to Know How Smart Animals Are?

## Summary

Frans de Waal challenges the idea that human intelligence is uniquely advanced, presenting evidence of animal cognition that blurs the line between human and animal minds.

## Key Takeaways

- Animal cognition research has repeatedly surprised us with how smart animals are.
- The boundary between human and animal intelligence is blurrier than we assumed.
- Recognizing animal minds changes our ethical obligations toward them.
