# Hamilton: The Revolution

## Summary

Lin-Manuel Miranda and Jeremy McCarter chronicle the creation of Hamilton, from initial idea to Broadway phenomenon, revealing the creative and historical research behind the revolutionary musical.

## Key Takeaways

- Hamilton proved that hip-hop could carry the weight of American history.
- The creative process involved years of research and multiple complete rewrites.
- Lin-Manuel Miranda's approach was to find the contemporary relevance in historical material.
