# When Breath Becomes Air

## Summary

Neurosurgeon Paul Kalanithi reflects on facing terminal cancer at age 36, weaving his experience of becoming a doctor with confronting death. The memoir grapples with what makes life meaningful when the future is taken away.

## Key Takeaways

- Facing death strips away everything non-essential and reveals what truly matters.
- Meaning can be created even when the future is taken away.
- The doctor-patient relationship is transformed when the doctor becomes the patient.
