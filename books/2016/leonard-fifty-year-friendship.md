# Leonard: My Fifty-Year Friendship with a Remarkable Man

## Summary

William Shatner shares five decades of friendship with Leonard Nimoy, tracing their bond from Star Trek's early days through their later lives. The book is a meditation on friendship, aging, and artistic legacy.

## Key Takeaways

- Deep friendships require intentional maintenance over decades.
- Shared creative work creates bonds that transcend ordinary relationships.
- Public figures often have richer private emotional lives than their personas suggest.
