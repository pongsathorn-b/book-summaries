# Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

## Summary

Yuval Noah Harari traces the history of Homo sapiens from the Stone Age to the present, arguing that the power of storytelling through shared myths is what separates us from other animals.

## Key Takeaways

- The Cognitive Revolution gave humans the power of shared fictional beliefs.
- The Agricultural Revolution was a catastrophic trap for most individuals.
- The Scientific Revolution revealed our ignorance and gave us unprecedented power.
