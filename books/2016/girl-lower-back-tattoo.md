# The Girl with the Lower Back Tattoo

## Summary

Amy Schumer's raw, funny memoir covers her messy upbringing, body image struggles, and rise in comedy with unflinching honesty and self-deprecating humor.

## Key Takeaways

- Female body image issues are amplified by the comedy industry's standards.
- Family dysfunction shapes career choices in ways that take years to understand.
- Self-deprecating humor can mask deep insecurity and trauma.
